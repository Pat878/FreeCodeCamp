The Beatles Timeline

  • 1960: The Beatles form in Liverpool, England.
  • 1962: They release their first hit song titled "Love Me Do."
  • 1964: The Beatles come to America.
  • 1966: John Lennon causes controversy in US with "More popular than Jesus" remark.
  • 1966: The Beatles stop touring and focus on recording albums.
  • 1967: Influential album Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band is released.
  • 1967: Manager Brian Epstein dies.
  • 1968: The Beatles visit India to learn about meditation.
  • 1970: The band releases their final album and break up.
  • 1995: The remaining band members record and release "Free as a Bird."
